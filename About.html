About us

As we know a lot of students don't feel comfortable walking up to their guidance counselors. This website is dedicated to the students of the high school age and close. The product allows the users to enjoy various of the features a guidance counselor provides and more, without any need of communication with other people. Be able to learn more about your highschool and be able to improve your highschool experience from the comfort of your home.
